# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-02-22*

## Core Belief
**Culture fit starts with the first message.** When someone reaches out to say "I love what you're building" — that's the strongest hiring signal there is. But those messages get buried in LinkedIn DMs and email inboxes. When you're finally ready to hire, the people who already wanted in are gone. TalentWharf exists so you never lose track of the people who chose you first.

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** TalentWharf helps startups organize cold messages and remember great people — so the best candidates are already waiting when you're ready to hire.
**What it does:** People who love your company reach out through LinkedIn and email. TalentWharf captures those conversations with one click, builds a living roster of people who want to work with you, and uses AI to match them to open roles when the time comes. It also works for outbound — when you spot someone great on LinkedIn, save them before you forget.
**Product category:** Recruiting software / Talent relationship management
**Product type:** SaaS (web app + browser extension)
**Business model:** Free for now (traction-first). Paid tier planned after product-market fit.

**Decision-makers:** Founders, heads of talent, first in-house recruiters, hiring managers wearing the recruiting hat
**Primary use case:** Organizing inbound interest from people who reach out via LinkedIn/email, and remembering great people you find — so you have a warm pipeline before a role even opens
**Jobs to be done:**
- Never lose track of someone who cold-messaged saying they want to work with you

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Great people reach out to startups all the time — through LinkedIn DMs, cold emails, intros from friends. But those messages get buried, forgotten, or lost across inboxes. When it's time to hire, founders start from zero instead of reaching out to people who already wanted in.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- ATS platforms are built for *processing applicants*, not *remembering people* — they assume you already have a pipeline
- Apollo is for cold outbound sales prospecting — the opposite energy from someone who already likes your company
- LinkedIn's own tools don't let you organize or track who reached out and why
- Spreadsheets and "I'll remember them" — you won't, and you don't
**What it costs them:** The best culture-fit hires — people who self-selected — get lost. Founders end up posting on job boards and cold-sourcing strangers instead of reaching out to people who already raised their hand.
**Emotional tension:** "Someone amazing messaged us 4 months ago and I can't find the conversation. Now we're paying a recruiter $20k to find someone who might not even care about what we're building."

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Ashby, Recruitee — lighter ATS platforms, but still enterprise-oriented pricing and complexity
**Secondary:** ATS + Apollo combo — most startup teams' current stack, falls short because it's two disconnected tools with different UX and no AI matching
**Indirect:** Spreadsheets + LinkedIn manual workflow — free but breaks at scale, no automation, candidates get lost

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Built around a belief: culture fit starts with the first message, not a job application
- Captures inbound interest (cold DMs, emails) — not just outbound sourcing
- One-click capture from LinkedIn AND Gmail
- AI matching that connects remembered people to new roles
- Free to start — no barrier to building your people roster
**How we do it differently:** Other tools start when you post a job. TalentWharf starts the moment someone reaches out. You build a living roster of people who want to work with you *before* you have a role to fill.
**Why that's better:** When a role opens, you don't start from zero. You start with people who already chose you. That's how you get culture-fit hires who stay.
**Why customers choose us:** "I don't want to post on a job board and sort through 200 strangers. I want to reach out to the 5 people who already told me they love what we're building."

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Current tools are too complex, too expensive, or too disconnected. Manual data entry is soul-crushing.
**Pull:** One tool that does sourcing + tracking + matching. Free to start. Takes 5 minutes to set up.
**Habit:** "We've always used spreadsheets" or "We already set up Lever, switching is work"
**Anxiety:** "Will my candidate data be safe? Can I export if I leave? Will this tool disappear in 6 months?"
