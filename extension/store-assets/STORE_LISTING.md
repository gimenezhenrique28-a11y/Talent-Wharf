## Full description (up to 16,000 chars)

**Stop copy-pasting. Start hiring.**

TalentWharf is a Chrome extension for recruiters that captures candidate profiles from LinkedIn and Gmail with a single click — saving them directly to your private talent pipeline.

---

### How it works

1. Browse to any LinkedIn profile or open an email in Gmail
2. Click the TalentWharf icon in your toolbar
3. Review the extracted data (name, headline, skills, experience)
4. Click **Add to Wharf** — done

The candidate is instantly saved to your TalentWharf dashboard, ready for pipeline management, outreach, and collaboration.

---

### What gets captured

**From LinkedIn profiles:**
- Full name
- Professional headline
- About / summary
- Skills (up to 20)
- Work experience
- Profile URL

**From Gmail:**
- Sender name
- Email address

---

### Privacy & data

- **Manual only** — the extension never runs in the background or captures anything automatically
- **Your data stays yours** — candidates are saved to your private workspace, not shared with anyone
- **No tracking** — we don't use analytics, ads, or third-party trackers
- **Secure** — your API key is stored locally by Chrome and transmitted only over HTTPS

Read our full privacy policy at https://app.talentwharf.com/privacy

---

### Getting started

1. Install the extension
2. Create a free account at https://app.talentwharf.com
3. Go to **Settings → API Keys** and generate a key
4. Open the extension options (right-click → Options) and paste your key
5. Browse to a LinkedIn profile and click **Add to Wharf**
